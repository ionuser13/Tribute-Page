Avicii

Dance music and popular culture icon

Here is a time line of Avicii's life:

  • 1989 - Born in Stockholm, Sweden.
  • 2007 - Signs with the Dejfitts Plays label.
  • 2009 - Release his remixes "Sound of now", "Muja", "Ryu" and "Even".
  • 2010 - Release "Seek bromance"(which reaches the top 20 in several countries) and a "Rapture" remix. Signs European A&R team with EMI Music Publishing.
  • 2011 - Release "Levels", which brought him into the mainstream.
  • 2012 - He is nominated for a Grammy for "Sunshine", a collaboration with David Guetta. Release a "Girl Gone Wild" remix and "Superlove" (ft. Lenny Kravitz) in the UMF edition of that year, "Two Million", "Silhouettes", "Dancing in my Head", "I'll be gone", "Let it go", "I Could Be the One" (ft., Nicky Romero). He participated in the Lollapalooza festival in Chicago.
  • 2013 - Collaborates with Ericsson to create a crowdsourced song. The result was "X You". Nominated for a Grammy thanks to "Levels". He tours Australia and part of South America. He release his album "True", as well as the singles "Wake Me Up", "Hey Brother" and "Addicted to You".
  • 2014 - He collaborates with Carlos Santana, Wycleg Jean and Alexandre Pires on "Dar um jeito (We will find a way)", the official anthem of the FIFA World Cup. Also with Chris Martin (Coldplay) in "A Sky Full of Stars", with David Guetta in "Lovers on the Sun" and with Wyclef Jean in "Divine sorrow", as well as with Jon Bon Jovi and Serj Tankian (System of a Down). Release "True: Avicii by Avicii", a remixed version of his album "True". Release the singles "Lay me down", "No pleasing a woman", "In love with your ghost", "Love to Fall", "Million Miles" (ft. LP), "Lose Myself" (ft. Wang Leehom) and "The Days" (ft. Robbie Williams).
  • 2015 - He presents his single "Heaven" (ft. Chris Martin) at the Future Music Festival in Australia. This was not released until 2019. It features "I'll Be Gone" on a Tiesto podcast. It would not be released until 2019. Release "Feeling Good", "Waiting for Love", "Track of My Tears", "Sorry Mr. Atari", "Time to Get III", "Broken Arrows" (ft. Zac Brown Band), Can't Catch Me (ft. Wyclef Jean and Matisyahu), "For a Better Day" (ft. Alex Ebert) and "Pure Grinding". He releases his album "Stories".
  • 2016 - Release a remix of "Beautiful heartbeat" and "Feeling good", "Feeling good (Avicii by Avicii)". He collaborates with Coca-Cola on "Taste the Feeling" (ft. Conrad Sewell), with Coldplay in "Hymn for the Weekend" and with Otto Knows in "Back where I belong". At the 2016 Ultra Music Festival he premieres "Without you" (ft. Sandro Cavazza), "We burn (Faster than light)", a collaboration with Sia. He retires from presentations and tours.
  • 2017 - He release "Lonely Together", a single from the Avici (01) album. The latter was released in August of that year. He stated that it was the first part of his third album. He announced a documentary titled "Avicii: True stories", which chronicles his retirement and features interviews with David Guetta, Tiesto, Chris Martin, among others of his colleagues.
  • 2018 - Release "Ghost" (ft. Daniel Adams-Ray). He passes away in Marcate, Oman. It was revealed that he had over 200 unreleased songs (finished and in development) at the time of his death.
  • 2019 - "Tim" is announced, the third album he was working on before his death. The official biography of him by MÃ¥ns Mosesson is also announced. The singles "SOS" and "Tough Love" are released, as well as the official video for the latter.
